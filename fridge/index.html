Fridgesque

SmrtrFridge is a refrigerator thermostat that helps users reach the optimal temperature range for whatever is in their fridge. Working closely with three other team members, we sought out to design a user-friendly thermostat that targets many problems users encounter today.

Research

We surveyed a group of people and the results showed us that 62% of them cared solely about convenience. They don't know why the temperature needs to change therefore they don't want to have to worry about it. Next, 30% of them claim that their food goes bad because they don't know the optimal environment and that they assume colder is better. Lastly, our survey showed that 8% care more abut their food than the fridge's energy consumption and they expect modern refrigerators to be efficient.
