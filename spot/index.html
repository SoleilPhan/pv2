Spot

This is a current project. Check updates regularly!

The goal of Spot is to minimize the inefficiencies that revolve around leasing parking spots. Currently, leased parking spaces are not used by the owner at all times and as a result, it is preventing others from finding a convenient space. When commuters are not occupying their leased parking space, there is no way for them to potentially offer their space to someone who may need it in the area. This opens up an opportunity for our solution to help commuters of all types (owners and non-owners of leased parking spots) to efficiently utilize a parking spot.
